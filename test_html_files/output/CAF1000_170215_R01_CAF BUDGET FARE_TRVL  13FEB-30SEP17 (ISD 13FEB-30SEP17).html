Conditions & Restrictions for Budget Fares

Effective

13-Feb-17

Sale

13 February 2017 - 30 September 2017

Travel

13 February 2017 - 30 September 2017

Seasonality

Fares based on all year round levels without seasonality.

Blackout

S-Class blackout dates: 27-31 March 2015, 05-26 December 2015, 03-15 January 2016, 21-25 March 2016 and 05-26 December 2016.

Discount

Children pay 75% of the adult fare

Infants pay 10% of the adult fare. If the Infant turns 2 years at any given time during the travel journey , Child fare must be applied from the point of origin.

Infant date of birth must be entered in Endorsement Box as: INF DOB YY/MM/DD

Fare Basis

Economy Class - YITX. Business Class - JITX

Fare Box

IT

Tourcode

CAF1000FF500

Tax & Surcharge

HKG security charge is excluded in the fares. Please indicate 'Q4.23' per HKG stopover or transit in the fare calculation box on the ticket. Fuel Surcharge is to be added.

Ticketing

Open-dated return not allowed. ET mandatory on all ET eligible tickets

Endorsements

Non Endorsable/Non Refundable / Non Reroutable

Combinations

Mixed class of travel permitted under the same tourcode in all cabins on half round trip basis. Open Jaws permitted under the same tourcode only.

Rerouting

Permitted charge USD100

Rerouting

1) Reissued to change one way journey to round trip, or change round trip to one way,

definitions

2) Reissued to cancel or add one sector,

Rebooking Fee

N/S/V/L/E/R/W class: USD50 same booking class otherwise upgrade to next higher fare. Show fee as OD TAX. M/K/H/B class: FOC in same booking class otherwise upgrade to next higher fare.

Refund Policy

75% of the fare paid prior to start of travel. No refund on partly used ticket.

Restrictions

All fare levels allowed on CX or KA or CX/KA codeshare flights

KUL & PEN = Not permitted on CX/MH codeshare flights; i.e. CX1724/CX1725/CX1726/CX1727

SGN = Not permitted on CX/VN codeshare flights; i.e. CX1762/CX1763

HAN = Not permitted on CX/VN codeshare flights; i.e. CX1790/CX1791

BKI = Not permitted on KA/MH codeshare flights; i.e. KA1384/KA1385

NAN = Only permitted on CX6900 and 6901

JPN = Not permitted on the following CX flight sequence CX6320-6323

TYO valid to HANEDA APT (HND) & NRT T2 APT(NRT)-Change between APT not classified as re-routing.

PG FLIGHT RESTRICTION: PG 3300-3399, PG3500-3699, PG4000-4999, PG6101-6999 and PG8000-8999

Others

Maximum 4 (FOUR) sectors permitted per pax.

Budget fares may only be issued in conjunction with CX longhaul beyond HKG.

Budget fares are not filed and will need to be manually fare built.
