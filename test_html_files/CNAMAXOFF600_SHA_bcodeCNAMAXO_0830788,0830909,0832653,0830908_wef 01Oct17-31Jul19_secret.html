    2. The BCODE, as an unique ticketing business code assigned for the Customer, MUST be put at the beginning of the “Endorsement/Restriction” box (EI: BCODECNAMAXOZ NONEND……)

Any tickets without the BCODE or in wrong format will be excluded from this incentive scheme. Cathay Pacific and/or Cathay Dragon (as the case may be) will not credit back the revenue for the tickets without the BCODE due to the mishandling of the handling agent.


    4. This corporate deal applies to air travels of Eligible Travelers only. “Eligible Travellers” are the employees, consultants, recruits, directors and clients of the Customer or any of its affiliates or subsidiaries travelling on company-related business, together with family members of any of the foregoing travelling on company-related business or pursuant to a relocation package, in each case whose travel is being paid for and by the Customer.


    5. Tickets of this offer and/or with BCODE MUST be reserved and issued through the appointed agent of listed in Appendix A.


    6. Cathay Pacific and/or Cathay Dragon (as the case may be) reserve the right to verify the tickets issuance under clause d) & e). In the event tickets of this offer and/or with BCODE reserved not by the appointed agent of listed in Clause A or issued to any ineligible travellers will be up debited to normal published fare level or CNY1000 penalty per ticket which is higher. Cathay Pacific and/or Cathay Dragon (as the case may be) also reserve the right to terminate the Agreement implementation by the agent.
